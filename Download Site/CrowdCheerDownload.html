Welcome to CrowdCheer!

CrowdCheer is an iOS app that helps spectators at races to cheer on runners when they need support.

1. Download CrowdCheer (You must be on your iPhone to download the app!)

Download

2. Go to your home screen to open the app.

3. You may see a message about "Untrusted Developer". This is because our app is for research and not on the App Store yet. Go to SETTINGS > GENERAL > DEVICE MANAGEMENT > NORTHWESTERN UNIVERSITY and Trust Northwestern to open the app.

4. Create an account and use the app to track your run or cheer for runners near you!

For more information, check out our lab site or send me an email with questions and concerns.
