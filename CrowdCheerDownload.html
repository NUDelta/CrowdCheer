Welcome to CrowdCheer!

Crowdsourcing Motivation for Runners

CrowdCheer is an iOS app that we're researching and developing in Delta Lab at Northwestern University. CrowdCheer gets the crowd of spectators at a race to cheer on runners when they need it most. For more information, check out our lab site or send me an email with questions and concerns.
